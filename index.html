Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A school or class for young children, usually four to six years old, that prepares them for first grade and that develops basic skills and social behavior by games, exercises, music, simple handicrafts, etc.

Elementary School

A school including usually the first four to the first eight grades and often a kindergarten.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school, also known as intermediate school, junior high school, junior secondary school, or lower secondary school, is an educational stage between primary school and secondary school.
